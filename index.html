Our Story

Founded in 2021, Sabores de España brings the authentic flavors of Spain to Uganda, Kampala. Our chief chef, Miguel Peña, hails from Barcelona and brings over 20 years of culinary experience to our kitchen.

We source only the finest ingredients, including imported Spanish olive oil, saffron, and paprika, to ensure an authentic dining experience.

Our warm, inviting atmosphere is designed to make you feel like you've stepped into a traditional Spanish taverna, right here in the heart of our Kampala.
